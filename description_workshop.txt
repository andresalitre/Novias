The Girlfriends Who Really Really Love You!

Adds the girlfriends who really REALLY love you as interactive NPCs, you can buy new items like weapons or potions with new effects. They will also give you new buffs when they accompany you!

Hakari Hanazono arrives after defeating the Eye of Cthulhu, she is really thirsty, help her drink a soda.

Karane Inda arrives after defeating the evil biome boss, she is looking for something small and soft to hug, apparently she likes kitties.

Shizuka Yoshimoto arrives after 2 girlfriends have moved in and the player has 5 books, apparently she lost her phone and is unable to talk to us.

Nano Eiai arrives after 2 girlfriends have moved in, she will be found scared on a sky island, go help her!
