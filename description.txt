The girlfriends who really Really love you in terraria!

Adds the girlfriends who really REALLY love you as interactive NPCs, you can buy new items like weapons or potions with new effects. They will also give you new buffs when they accompany you!

Hakari Hanazono arrives after defeating the Eye of Cthulhu, she is really thirsty, but she won't drink more than 175ml.

Karane Inda arrives after defeating the corruption boss of the world, she is looking for something small and soft to hug, apparently she likes kitties.
