The Girlfriends Who Really Really Love You!

Add The Girlfriends Who Really Really Love You! as interactive NPCs, each with unique missions (almost unique...)
The NPCs can follow the player and join them on their adventure, and you can also kiss them and obtain new buffs!

How to get started:

-Build a house for Hakari Hanazono, no additional conditions required.
-Karane Inda will arrive after completing Hakari Hanazono's first mission.
-As you progress through Hakari and Karane's missions, Shizuka Yoshimoto will arrive.
-As you complete Shizuka's missions, Nano Eiai will arrive in your world.
-In short: Complete missions to unlock all the girlfriends!
-More missions coming soon!
